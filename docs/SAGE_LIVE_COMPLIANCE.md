# Sage Live Sessions - GRC & Compliance Documentation

## ISO 42001 Alignment

Sage Live Sessions implements AI governance controls aligned with ISO 42001:2023 standards:

### 1. Transparency & Disclosure
- ✅ AI-generated content watermarking
- ✅ Sage Twin identity disclosure
- ✅ Voice and avatar licensing acknowledgment
- ✅ Clear creator/AI distinction in UI

### 2. Accountability
- ✅ Comprehensive audit logging
- ✅ Session recordings and transcripts
- ✅ Decision traceability
- ✅ Human-in-the-loop controls

### 3. Fairness & Non-discrimination
- ✅ Content filtering and moderation
- ✅ Bias detection mechanisms
- ✅ Equal access policies
- ✅ Inclusive design practices

### 4. Privacy & Data Protection
- ✅ Row Level Security (RLS)
- ✅ GDPR-compliant data handling
- ✅ User consent management
- ✅ Data minimization

### 5. Safety & Security
- ✅ Emergency stop functionality
- ✅ Safe mode toggle
- ✅ Content moderation layers
- ✅ Rate limiting and abuse prevention

## Compliance Features

### Content Moderation
- Three-tier filtering: Strict, Moderate, Relaxed
- Real-time AI response review
- Blocked/allowed topic lists
- Human approval workflows

### Audit Logging
All events are logged to `live_session_audit_logs`:
- Session start/end
- Sage Twin responses
- Content moderation flags
- Emergency stops
- Payment transactions

### Emergency Controls
- Immediate session termination
- Viewer disconnection
- AI Sage Twin shutdown
- Compliance notification system

### Licensing & Rights Management
- Voice sample licensing acknowledgment
- Avatar usage rights confirmation
- Automatic rights expiration tracking
- Revocation mechanisms

## Compliance Checklist

Before going live, creators must ensure:

1. ☐ Voice and avatar licenses acknowledged
2. ☐ Content filter level configured
3. ☐ Blocked topics defined (if applicable)
4. ☐ Human approval setting reviewed
5. ☐ Safe mode enabled (recommended)
6. ☐ Emergency stop procedure understood
7. ☐ Sage Twin testing completed
