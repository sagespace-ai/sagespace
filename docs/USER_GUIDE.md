## Getting Started

### What is SageSpace?

SageSpace is an AI platform where you interact with specialized AI agents called **Sages**. Each Sage is an expert in a specific domain - from creative writing to data analysis to philosophical debate.

What makes SageSpace unique:
- **Self-Healing**: The platform detects and fixes errors automatically
- **User-Evolving**: The AI observes your patterns and suggests improvements
- **You're in Control**: No changes happen without your approval
- **Domain-Specialized Sages**: Each AI stays in its expertise area

### Your First Steps

1. **Sign Up** - Create your account at the landing page
2. **Complete Genesis Chamber** - Quick onboarding to set preferences
3. **Visit the Hub** - Your dashboard and starting point
4. **Choose a Sage** - Browse the Multiverse or use the Playground
5. **Start Chatting** - Ask questions, get answers, explore

## Working with Sages

### Choosing the Right Sage

Each Sage specializes in specific domains. Match your need to their expertise:

- **Creative Sages**: Writing, art, music, storytelling
- **Technical Sages**: Code, math, engineering, data
- **Strategic Sages**: Business, planning, decision-making
- **Social Sages**: Communication, relationships, culture
- **Learning Sages**: Education, tutoring, skill development

### Getting Better Responses

**Do:**
- Be specific about what you need
- Provide context
- Ask follow-up questions
- Use mood selector for tone adjustment

**Don't:**
- Ask Sages to work outside their domain
- Expect instant expertise in everything
- Share sensitive personal information

### Domain Boundaries

Sages are designed to stay within their expertise. If you ask something outside their domain:
- They'll acknowledge limitations
- Suggest a better-suited Sage
- Offer to escalate to Council

This is a **feature, not a bug** - specialization creates better answers.

## The Adaptive System

### How SageSpace Evolves

SageSpace includes an AI system called the **Dreamer** that:
1. Observes how you use the platform
2. Identifies patterns and friction points
3. Generates improvement proposals
4. Submits them for your review

### Reviewing Proposals

Go to **Settings ‚Üí Adaptive Mode** to see AI-generated proposals.

**For each proposal you can:**
- **Approve**: Apply the change immediately
- **Reject**: Decline and help AI learn your preferences
- **Ask Why**: See detailed reasoning

**What might be proposed:**
- UI layout adjustments
- Navigation shortcuts for your frequent tasks
- Sage recommendations based on your patterns
- Feature toggles based on your usage

### Design Karma

Reviewing proposals earns you **Design Karma** points:
- +10 points per review
- +20 points for thoughtful feedback
- Level up to unlock "Architect" badge
- Track your streak in Observatory

### Governance & Safety

All proposals pass through **10 strict governance policies** before reaching you:

1. No removal of safety features
2. No data exfiltration
3. Preserve legal requirements
4. Respect privacy settings
5. Maintain core functionality
6. No breaking changes
7. No manipulative patterns
8. Fair monetization only
9. Respect Sage domains
10. No hallucinated features

**You'll never see proposals that:**
- Remove safety features
- Compromise your privacy
- Break existing functionality
- Manipulate your behavior

## FAQ

### General

**Q: What makes SageSpace different from ChatGPT?**  
A: SageSpace features domain-specialized Sages (not one general AI), multi-Sage councils, a self-evolving system that learns your preferences, and full user control over all changes.

**Q: Is my data private?**  
A: Yes. We use Row Level Security (RLS) in the database. You control sharing. See Privacy Policy for details.

**Q: How much does it cost?**  
A: Free tier includes 10 daily interactions. Pro plan ($9.99/mo) offers 100 daily interactions and priority access.

### Using Sages

**Q: Why won't my Sage answer something?**  
A: Sages stay within their domain. If they decline, try a different Sage or escalate to Council.

**Q: Can I create unlimited custom Sages?**  
A: Free tier allows 3 custom Sages. Pro tier allows 20. Team tier is unlimited.

**Q: How do I delete a conversation?**  
A: In Memory, hover over the conversation and click the delete icon. Deleted conversations cannot be recovered.

### Adaptive System

**Q: Will the platform change without my permission?**  
A: Never. All changes require your explicit approval in Adaptive Mode.

**Q: What if I don't like an approved change?**  
A: Return to Adaptive Mode and toggle off the feature flag. Changes are reversible.

**Q: How often should I review proposals?**  
A: Check weekly. The Dreamer generates proposals based on accumulated usage patterns.

### Technical

**Q: What AI models power the Sages?**  
A: We use Vercel AI Gateway with multiple providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq) for reliability and performance.

**Q: Can I use SageSpace offline?**  
A: No, SageSpace requires an internet connection.

**Q: Do you support mobile?**  
A: Yes, the web app is fully responsive. Native mobile apps coming soon.

### Troubleshooting

**Q: My chat isn't responding**  
A: Refresh the page. If the issue persists, check Settings ‚Üí Integrations to ensure AI providers are connected.

**Q: I lost my conversation history**  
A: History is saved automatically. If missing, contact support with your session details.

**Q: Feature flag broke something**  
A: Toggle it off in Settings ‚Üí Adaptive Mode. If the issue persists, contact support.
